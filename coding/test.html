About me

My passion for working as a creative and designer is driven by my appreciation for good problem solving. I beliefe that developing ideas, that break down expert knowledge and insights into easy to understand chunks of information, are the main responsibility of a creative and art director. However my passion is problem solving - digging deep into a problem, finding the core issue and developing the right approach to solve it. My way of visualizing solutions is to establish a sophisticated and meaningful design. I consider every element with it's own functionally and purpose. Form follows function. Therefore I can present my work and justify every doubt with clear statements. I wrote a book, "Stay edgy, be dainty", for interns and rookies in the graphic and advertising industry. The main part of „Stay edgy, be dainty“ is a guideline in chronological order, helping future interns and rookies with my experience and advice from advertising specialists. Starting off by helping how to find the right graphic design bureau or advertising agency, I accompany the student during the entire progress of their internship.
